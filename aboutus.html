Our Mission:

To give the students at Quinnipiac a community to improve their technical skills!

History

Established when Quinnipiacs School of Engineering was established. The computing club was established as a faculty led club that aimed to help students in the computer science or software engineering program. Later in its life, it was extended to anyone who was interested in computing. The club now, is student run by four executive board members who elect those individuals every year. The club is primarily for undergraduates, however graduates and alumni are always welcome as the computing club, and the overall Quinnipiac Engineering program, are an inclusive community with a close culture.

What we do

We enjoying a range of events, which can be found here.

  • Hackathons
  • Coding tutorials
  • Barbecues
  • Hacking tutorials
  • Homework Help!

Member Expectations

As a member of the computing club, you will be encouraged to attend all the meetings. This will allow you to stay up to date with all of the upcoming events and different goals of the club. You will also need to attend all of the events to shows presence and promote the club to the highest standards. Event attendance and participation is crucial to the success of the club as a whole as it will allow the community and unity experience of other participants.

Meetings

Attending meetings is very important and key to the success of the club. Member attendance will allow for all needed areas of the club to be discussed so that the club can be successful with all plans. Expect to go through different events and ideas to generate success for the club.
